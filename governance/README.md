## üõ°Ô∏è The "Human-in-the-Loop" (HITL) Protocol
The Shadow Identity Framework rejects "Prompt & Dump" automation. Every standard and policy in this repository follows the **SIF-HITL Protocol**, which ensures that the human architect remains the sole source of strategic logic.



1.  **Requirement Definition:** The Project Lead (Kathryn Wilson) defines the strategic threat model (e.g., "Securing a Spoke identity against SIM-swapping").
2.  **Inquiry & Scaffolding:** AI tools are used to retrieve industry standards (NIST/FIDO2) and scaffold GRC-compliant formatting.
3.  **Critical Refinement:** The Project Lead rewrites, paraphrases, and infuses the text with the SIF's unique architectural voice and unique requirements.
4.  **Physical Audit:** No standard is finalized until the Project Lead physically verifies the technical migration on hardware (e.g., YubiKey, M-DISC, or Linux-hardened environments).
