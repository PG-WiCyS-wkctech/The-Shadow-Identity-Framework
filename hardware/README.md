> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Warning on SSDs:** SSDs store data using electrical charges. If left unpowered in a drawer for more than 2 years, they can lose data ("Leakage"). **Do not use SSDs as your only long-term archive.** Use M-DISC for anything you want to keep for 10+ years.

## üîÑ Maintenance & Rotation Policy (GRC-HW-01)
To ensure compliance with the Shadow Identity Framework, follow this rotation:

* **Quarterly (The SSD Sync):** Plug in your SSD, sync all new photos/videos from the Hub (iCloud/Gmail), and verify files. This "refreshes" the electrical charge in the cells.
* **Annually (The M-DISC Burn):** Every January, burn the previous year's "Finalized" data to an M-DISC.
* **Geographic Separation:** Store your M-DISCs in a different physical location (e.g., a relative's house or a safety deposit box) to protect against fire/theft.
